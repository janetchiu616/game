Ten frame making ten game for young children

⭐ 0 stars

Fill the box to make 10! 🥚

There are 7 eggs. Tap the empty spots to fill them up.

Full spots: 7  ·  Empty spots: 3

For parents: This is the big one. As he fills the empty spots, say it out loud: "7 eggs, and 3 more makes 10!" The empty spots ARE the "friend of 10." Point at them before he fills: "How many empty? That's how many more we need."
